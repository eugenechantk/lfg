### 2026-07-01 — Subagent's navigation theory was wrong, contradicted the bug

**What happened:** The Explore subagent reported that after ending a session, "SwiftUI automatically clears the selection binding" and pops back to the list. That directly contradicted the reported bug (it does NOT return to the list). I didn't take the claim at face value — read RootView/SessionDetailView myself and found the real cause: `selection` keeps pointing at the deleted session, so the detail column falls into the `store.session(selection) == nil` branch and shows `DetailLoading` ("Opening session…") forever.
**Why it matters:** Subagent conclusions about framework behavior are hypotheses, not ground truth. When a subagent's claim contradicts the observed symptom, the symptom wins — verify against source.
**What better looks like:** Treat search-agent behavioral claims as leads; confirm the mechanism in source before acting. (Reinforces [[ground-truth-before-hypothesizing]].)
